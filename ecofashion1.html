Why EcoFashion?

We all want to do our part to help the environment, but often we don't know where to start. We recycle, we carpool, and we buy organic produce - but beyond that, it's tough to know what you else you can do, besides tying yourself to a tree in protest or sacrificing modern conveniences like the occasional paper plate or bottle of water (it would be rough to host a BBQ without them).

However, as our motivation to "go green" increases, so does the demand for environmentally-friendly products. At Lazy Glamour we feel that our green lifestyle should also extend to the clothes we wear. Wouldn't be nice to find garments that are manufactured in a way that reduces pollution, provides fair and equitable job opportunities, and is free from harmful chemicals? We thought so - especially for our underwear!
